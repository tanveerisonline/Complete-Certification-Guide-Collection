# CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) Guide (2025-2026)

## Overview
CISSP is the highest-level security certification validating expertise in information security architecture, design, and management across 8 domains.

**Certification Code:** CISSP (110-100)

---

## Duration & Study Timeline
- **Recommended Study Duration:** 6-12 months
- **Study Hours Required:** 300-500 hours
- **Experience Requirement:** 5+ years information security work

---

## Exam Details

### Exam Duration
- **Total Duration:** 6 hours (can be split into 2x3 hour sessions)
- **Total Questions:** 100-150 questions
- **Passing Score:** 70% minimum (roughly 70-105 questions correct)
- **Question Format:** Multiple choice, complex scenarios

### Exam Fee (2025-2026)
- **Exam Fee:** $749 USD
- **Retake Fee:** $749 USD
- **Membership Fee:** $125/year (after certification)
- **Total First Year:** ~$874

## Certification Validity

### Validity Period
- **3-year cycle** from certification date
- **Renewal requirements:**
  - 120 Continuing Education credits
  - Professional membership
  - Annual fees ($125/year)
