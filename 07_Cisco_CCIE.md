# Cisco CCIE Routing & Switching Certification Guide (2025-2026)

## Overview
Cisco CCIE (Cisco Certified Internetwork Expert) is the highest-level Cisco certification, validating expert-level networking knowledge and hands-on lab skills in designing, implementing, and troubleshooting complex networks.

**Certification Code:** CCIE Routing & Switching (Written 350-901, Lab 8100)

---

## Duration & Study Timeline
- **Recommended Study Duration:** 12-24 months
- **Study Hours Required:** 800-1200 hours
- **Hands-On Lab Time:** 400-600 hours
- **Total Commitment:** 1-2 years minimum

---

## Exam Details

### Written Exam (350-901)
- **Duration:** 120 minutes
- **Questions:** 60-70 items
- **Passing Score:** 804/1000
- **Fee:** $400 USD
- **Format:** Multiple choice, drag-and-drop, simulation

### Lab Exam (8100)
- **Duration:** 8 hours
- **Format:** Hands-on lab exercises
- **Stations:** 4-5 lab scenarios
- **Passing Score:** 67%+
- **Fee:** $600 USD
- **Available:** Limited dates, must register in advance

### Total Cost
- **Two-Exam Path:** $1,000 USD (lab fees vary by location)
- **Study Materials:** $500-2,000+ depending on resources
- **Retake Lab Exam:** $600 USD per attempt

## Hands-On Lab Requirements

### Lab Platforms
- **Cisco Modeling Labs (CML)** - Most realistic (commercial)
  - Real Cisco device images
  - Full topology control
  - Networking simulations
  - Cost: $500-1500/year
  
- **GNS3** - Open source alternative
  - Community support
  - Free with some limitations
  - Real device images available
  
- **EVE-NG** - Enterprise simulator
  - Professional environment
  - Large topology support
  
- **Cisco DevNet** - Online labs
  - Real device access (limited)
  - API learning
  - Free tier available

### Critical Lab Scenarios
1. **Multi-Protocol Routing**
   - OSPF multi-area
   - EIGRP with multiple vendors
   - BGP routing policies
   - Route manipulation
   - VRF implementations

2. **Service Provider Architectures**
   - MPLS deployment
   - BGP in SP environment
   - VPLS, L3VPN
   - Service offerings

3. **Security Implementation**
   - IPSec VPN at scale
   - Device hardening
   - Advanced ACLs
   - Threat defense

4. **High Availability**
   - Redundancy design
   - BFD implementation
   - Graceful restart
   - Stateful failover

5. **Network Automation**
   - Python scripting
   - Ansible playbooks
   - API integration
